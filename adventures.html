Banana Boatings

  • 30-Minute Ride: P350 per person

Inclusions:

  • Life Jackets: Safety is a priority. Life jackets are provided for all participants.
  • Professional Guides: Experienced guides ensure a safe and thrilling ride.
  • Group Fun: Banana boats can accommodate up to six passengers at a time.
  • Watersports Hub: Convenient access to all watersports equipment and facilities.
  • Photo Service: Capture your adventure with optional professional photography.
  • Beachside Views: Enjoy the ride with stunning coastal views and crystal-clear waters.
  • Reservations: Book your ride in advance to secure your spot.

Island Hoppings

  • Half-Day Tour (4 hours): P600 per person
  • Full-Day Tour (8 hours): P1000 per person

Inclusions:

  • Experienced Guides: Knowledgeable guides to lead you to the most beautiful and remote islands.
  • Private Boat: Travel in comfort and style on a private boat with seating for your group.
  • Snorkeling Gear: Explore vibrant underwater worlds with complimentary snorkeling gear.
  • Picnic Lunch: Enjoy a delicious picnic lunch on a secluded beach.
  • Swim Stops: Opportunities to swim, sunbathe, and relax at various picturesque spots.
  • Island Exploration: Discover hidden coves, caves, and wildlife on uninhabited islands.
  • Refreshments: Stay hydrated with provided water, soft drinks, and tropical fruit.
  • Safety Equipment: Life vests and first aid kits on board for your safety.
  • Photo Opportunities: Capture memorable moments with your camera or smartphone.
  • Flexible Itineraries: Customizable trips to suit your preferences and schedule.
