Billiards Game

  • Non-Resort Guest Rate: For visitors who are not staying at the resort, there is an hourly rate of P150 per hour to play billiards. This includes cue and ball rental.
  • Membership Package: The resort offers a special "Billiards Enthusiast" membership package for those who want unlimited billiards play during their stay. The package includes unlimited access to the billiards area and costs P250 for the entire duration of the stay.
  • Equipment Rental: If you'd like to play but don't have your own equipment, cue and ball rental is available for P50 per hour for non-resort guests.
